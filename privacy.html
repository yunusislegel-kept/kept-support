Privacy Policy

Your data stays on your Mac.

This Privacy Policy explains how Kept handles data. The short answer: Kept does not collect personal data, does not create accounts, does not use analytics, does not use advertising, and does not automatically sync clipboard history to a server.

Last updated: May 20, 2026
Contact Support Back to Support

1. Data collection

Kept does not collect, store, transmit, sell, rent, or share personal data with the developer. Kept does not require a user account, email address, username, login, subscription profile, or any other identifying account information to use the app.

2. Local clipboard history

Kept is a local-first macOS clipboard history app. Clipboard items, boards, searches, filters, rules, settings, pinned items, local snapshots, and backups are designed to remain on the user's Mac.

▣

Local Library container

Kept stores its app data locally inside the macOS Library/Application Support app container. The developer does not have access to this local container.

⌁

No cloud sync

Kept does not automatically synchronize clipboard history between devices. Moving data to another Mac requires a user-controlled manual export and import.

3. Network, analytics, ads, and tracking

Kept does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking technologies, or behavioral profiling tools. Kept does not upload clipboard content to remote servers for processing.

⌘

No network access for clipboard data

Clipboard capture, search, rules, local snapshots, and backups are designed to work on the Mac.

✕

No tracking

Kept does not track users across apps or websites and does not share data with advertising networks.

4. Supported content types

Depending on how you use the app, Kept may locally save supported clipboard content such as:

  • Text and links
  • Images and small previews
  • PDF clipboard items
  • Animated GIFs
  • Colors and color values
  • Contact cards copied by the user
  • Calendar event data copied by the user
  • Files or file references supported by macOS clipboard behavior

Kept is designed for clipboard productivity. Large audio and video content may be excluded or treated differently to keep the clipboard library fast and lightweight.

5. Sensitive content protection

Kept includes privacy features intended to reduce accidental exposure of sensitive clipboard items. These features are local to the app and can be adjusted by the user.

✋

Hidden copies

Copies that appear private or are marked as hidden can be hidden by default from normal views.

◇

Smart Secret Guard

Kept can detect common secret-like patterns such as API keys, bearer tokens, private keys, passwords, and similar sensitive values, then save them in a more protected way.

⏱

Short-lived sensitive items

Items marked as temporary or sensitive can be automatically removed after a short time, depending on the user's privacy settings.

◌

Private mode

Kept can temporarily pause capture before sensitive work, so clipboard changes are not read while private mode is active.

6. Protected sources and rules

Kept allows users to exclude or protect specific source applications and websites. For example, users may choose to avoid saving content copied from password managers, private messaging apps, terminal sessions, protected websites, or other sensitive sources.

7. Backups, exports, and imports

Kept does not automatically export or synchronize your clipboard history. Exports and backups are manual actions controlled by the user. If you choose to export Kept data, the exported file is your responsibility and should be stored securely.

8. Data deletion

Users can clear clipboard history, remove saved items, delete local backups, and reset saved data from inside the app where available. Users may also remove Kept's local macOS application container to delete local Kept data from the Mac.

9. Permissions

Kept is designed to avoid unnecessary permissions. The app does not require access to location, camera, microphone, contacts, calendar, or network services for its clipboard history functionality. Any content saved in Kept comes from user clipboard actions and macOS clipboard behavior.

10. Third-party services

Kept does not send clipboard history or personal data to third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or cloud-processing services.

11. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if Kept's features or data handling practices change. The latest version will be available on this page.
