About Kept

Kept helps you keep useful clipboard items close at hand without turning your clipboard into a cloud service. You can capture text, links, colors, PDFs, GIFs, contact cards, calendar events, and other supported content types; search them later; pin important items; organize them by source or session; and paste them back when needed.

Privacy by design

⌘

No network access

Kept does not need an internet connection for its core features. Clipboard history, boards, rules, settings, and backups are designed to remain on the Mac.

◌

No account or login

Kept does not ask for an email address, username, account, or profile. There is no server-side user profile to create, delete, or maintain.

▣

Local Library storage

Kept stores app data locally in the user's macOS Library container. Removing the app's Library container removes the app's saved local data.

⇧

Manual export

Kept does not automatically sync your history. Exports and backups are controlled by the user and are intended for moving data between Macs manually.

✋

Hidden sensitive items

Kept can hide copies that appear private or are marked as hidden. You can adjust this behavior in the app's privacy settings.

◇

Sandboxed workspace

Kept runs inside the macOS App Sandbox and does not request unnecessary permissions such as location, contacts, calendar, camera, microphone, or network access.

Features

⌕

Search everything

Search copied text, filenames, source apps, colors, and supported content types using focused filters such as text, image, file, color, source, date, and pinned state.

☷

Paste queue

Build a queue of copied items and paste them in order. This is useful for forms, messages, repeated replies, and structured workflows.

📌

Pinned items

Pin important clips so they stay available even when normal clipboard history is cleaned or limited by your plan.

▤

Menu bar library

Keep your clipboard library available from the menu bar, quick panel, or main app window depending on how you prefer to work.

▦

Sessions and filters

Kept groups nearby copies by time and source app, making it easier to understand where content came from and when it was captured.

↺

Backups and snapshots

Kept can create local snapshots that you can restore from. Backups remain local unless you choose to export or move them yourself.

Frequently asked questions

Does Kept collect personal data?

No. Kept does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data with the developer.

Does Kept sync between devices?

No. Kept has no automatic cloud synchronization. You can manually export and import your history when needed.

Where is my history stored?

Your Kept history and settings are stored locally inside your Mac's application container.

Can I delete my Kept data?

Yes. You can clear history inside the app. You can also remove Kept's local application container from your Mac to remove all local Kept data.

Does Kept upload clipboard content?

No. Kept is designed so clipboard capture, search, rules, and backups stay on your Mac.
